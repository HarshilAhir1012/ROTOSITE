About Rotaract Club LJIET

Rotaract Club LJIET is a vibrant community of young leaders committed to creating positive change through service, fellowship, and leadership development. As part of the global Rotaract network, we focus on community service, international understanding, and professional development.

Our club has been actively serving the community for over 8 years, organizing impactful projects in areas of health, education, environment, and social welfare. We believe in the power of collective action and strive to make a meaningful difference in society.
